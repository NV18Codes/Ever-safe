FRP Pressure Vessels

FRP (Fiberglass Reinforced Plastic) Pressure Vessels are specially engineered for use in water filtration and softening systems. These vessels are corrosion-resistant, lightweight, and highly durable, capable of withstanding high pressure in industrial environments. Ideal for use in commercial water plants, homes, hotels, and manufacturing setups.

  • NSF-certified materials
  • Operating pressure up to 10.5 bar
  • Long service life and minimal maintenance

Water Softening Systems

Our Water Softening Systems operate on ion-exchange principles to eliminate calcium and magnesium ions from water, preventing scaling in pipelines and improving cleaning efficiency. These systems are widely used in households, hotels, laundries, and industries where water quality is critical.

  • Automated and manual regeneration options
  • Corrosion-proof construction
  • Highly efficient cation resins used

Membrane Housings

Membrane housings are high-performance pressure vessels engineered for RO, UF, and NF membrane installations. They protect and support the membranes during high-pressure operations and are available in FRP and stainless steel variants. SS membrane housings are preferred in hygienic and high-pressure applications.

  • Available in 2.5" , 4", and 8" diameters
  • Side and end port configurations for flexible piping
  • Pressure ratings up to 1200 PSI
  • Stainless steel options for enhanced durability and sanitation

Multiport Valves

Multiport Valves (MPVs) are vital for managing multiple operations such as backwash, rinse, and filtration in a single valve. These valves are offered in both manual and automatic versions for diverse applications in domestic, commercial, and industrial systems.

  • Constructed from high-grade ABS and brass
  • Manual MPVs use a lever for each cycle; ideal for basic installations
  • Automatic MPVs offer timer- or meter-based control for precision and convenience
  • Top and side mount configurations for ease of integration

Swimming Pool Filtration Systems

Our Swimming Pool Filtration Systems ensure crystal-clear water through efficient sand filtration. These systems include corrosion-resistant fiberglass tanks and optional square brine tanks for backwashing and chlorination systems in high-end applications.

  • Uses isophthalic resin for UV and chemical resistance
  • Top-mount or side-mount MPVs for user-friendly operation
  • Square brine tanks available for enhanced salt distribution and footprint optimization
  • Ideal for residential, club, and resort pools
